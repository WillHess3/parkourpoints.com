Athlete of the Month

George Westerlund

George Westerlund has been a fierce competitor from a young age and showed he still has what it takes when he placed first at the Hub Speed Comp. George had the course record on all three courses and finished over three and a half seconds faster than second place. George is not affiliated with any gym and does not have as many opportunities to train as other competitors making his victory in the 14+ division even more impressive! George is truly a force to be reckoned with!
